Castle Conquerors

Are you ready, adventurer?

Game Rules

Come and conquer! Climb tall mountains to get a chance to have a better look on surrounding areas, get through high grass in search of a treasure or an enemy's castle and avoid lakes full of piranhas that will eat you instantly.



Find and use your treasure to bribe castle's guards and steal crown of your enemy before they got a chance to do it faster than you!
