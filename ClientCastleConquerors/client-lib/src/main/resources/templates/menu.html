Castle Conquerors

Are you ready, adventurer?

Game Rules

Come and conquer! Climb tall mountains to get a chance to have a better look on surrounding areas, get through high grass in search of a treasure or an enemy's castle and avoid lakes full of piranhas that will eat you instantly.



Use your found treasure to bribe castle's guards and steel crown of your enemy before they got a chance to do it before you!

Map tiles:

Green
(grassland)		 movement 1 move to get in
1 move to get out
visibility while inside, you can see only what is inside of this field
special May contain a treasure or a castle

Gray
(mountain)		 movement 2 moves to get in
2 moves to get out
visibility while inside, you can see what is inside of this field and nearby tiles
special You can see what is inside neighbour tiles: you will see all treasures and castles that are in 3x3 square (your position is in the centre)

Blue
(water)		 movement 1 move to get in
visibility none
special instant loss
